IT'S ALL ABOUT WHAT YOU CAN ACHIEVE

Empower yourself to make the changes you need to make

Cardio
Every Day 8:00 PM; A solid cardio workout is the backbone of an effective workout regime, which is why we run cardio classes twice daily. Whether you are right at the start of a weight loss journey, or simply looking to tone up and keep fit, our cardio classes will put you through your paces and keep you on top form
AirYoga
Every Wednesday and Saturday 8:00 AM; Sure, you have heard or regular yoga, but what about trying out some new moves while floating feet into the air? Air yoga is an interdisciplinary workout that combines the flexibility and technique of regular yoga with the strength building of hanging. Whether you have tried air yoga before or are a total beginner, you are welcome in our classes.
Lifting
Every Day 21:00; Lifting is not just about strength. While you will build great core, arm, and leg muscles while lifting, you will also develop better coordination and an understanding of how your body interacts with the world. Whether you are already an experienced weightlifter, or have never picked up a dumbbell in your life, you will find a lot of value in our classes.
Press & back
Every Day 10:00 AM; There are few better full-body workouts available than our press and back classes. We welcome members of all abilities to our classes, and are always keen to help beginners make a start If you can not find a class at a time that suits you, please get in touch with us, since we often hold spontaneous classes which do not make their way onto the official schedule.
